Privacy Policy

How we handle your data

Last Updated: February 20, 2026

AtlasAI Inc. ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this Privacy Policy. This policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you visit the AtlasAI website or use our autonomous sales intelligence platform (the "Service"), and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our Service, including:

  • Account Information: Name, work email address, company name, and payment details when you register for an account.
  • Customer Data: Information you input into the Service, including target domains, email templates, and generated lead lists. As per our Terms of Service, you retain full ownership of this data.
  • Usage Data & Telemetry: Hardware performance metrics (e.g., local GPU utilization), navigation paths, and interaction data to help us reliably improve the system.
  • Extracted Data: Data scraped from public web sources using our AI agents, executed strictly via your instruction.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use information that we collect about you or that you provide to us:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • To process transactions and send related information, including confirmations and invoices.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your requests, comments, and inquiries.
  • To train and optimize our Large Language Models (LLMs) and routing heuristics (subject to strict anonymization and aggregation protocols, as outlined in our Terms of Service).
  • To monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our Service.

3. Privacy-First Architecture

AtlasAI is designed with a "Local-First" architecture. When you deploy our models locally, the actual inference and data processing occur on your own hardware. We do not extract your proprietary lead data to centralized servers unless explicitly required for a feature you enable (e.g., cloud fallback or shared workspaces).

4. AI Training and Aggregation

We utilize aggregated, non-personally identifiable telemetry and system usage patterns to enhance our models' ability to parse websites and draft effective outreach. We do not expose your specific, proprietary lead lists or exact customer communications to other AtlasAI customers.

5. Data Security

We have implemented measures designed to secure your personal information from accidental loss and from unauthorized access, use, alteration, and disclosure. However, the transmission of information via the internet is not completely secure. Although we do our best to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your transmission.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., under the GDPR or CCPA), you may have the right to request access to, correct, or delete any personal information that you have provided to us. You can usually access and update your information in your account settings. For specific data removal requests, please contact us.

7. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

It is our policy to post any changes we make to our privacy policy on this page. If we make material changes to how we treat our users' personal information, we will notify you by email to the primary email address specified in your account or through a notice on the Service home page.

8. Contact Information

To ask questions or comment about this privacy policy and our privacy practices, contact us at: contact@raytechnica.work or manjreu@gmail.com.
