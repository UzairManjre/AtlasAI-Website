9 — Landing page FAQ (use these exact Q&A)

Q: Does data leave my network?
A: By default the system runs on your machine and stores data locally (SQLite). If you opt into cloud services or ask us to host, only then will data be transmitted — and only with explicit agreement.

Q: Do I need a GPU?
A: No for basic scraping & small runs. For faster local LLM inference we recommend a 6–8GB GPU for comfortable performance; CPU-only works at slower speed.

Q: What models does it use?
A: The product is model-agnostic — we support local LLMs via a local LLM server such as Ollama. You can use smaller, fast models for vision/score and a larger marketing model for copy.

Q: Is scraping legal?
A: We follow robots.txt by default and provide configuration to respect site policies. Users must comply with local laws and best practices. We also provide guidance on ethical outreach.

Q: How long to set up?
A: With basic familiarity: ~1–2 hours for installation and model pulling. Our onboarding service (paid) handles the rest.

Q: What support do you offer?
A: Email support for all plans; priority and hands-on setup for Pro and enterprise customers.
